Privacy

This page describes the privacy boundaries implemented by SupportMemory. Deploying organisations remain responsible for publishing terms that reflect their legal entity, hosting region, subprocessors, retention policy, and customer agreements.

Private workspace data

Support conversations, knowledge sources, connector events, credentials, and operational records are processed within organisation and workspace boundaries. Connector credentials are encrypted before persistence.

Access and audit records

SupportMemory records operational actions needed to secure and audit the service. Retention is configured by the deploying organisation.

External providers

When an organisation enables an integration or model provider, relevant data is sent to that provider under the organisation’s configuration and agreement.

Deployment contact

Privacy requests should be directed to the organisation operating your SupportMemory deployment.
