Terms

SupportMemory is software for organisation-operated support workflows. The organisation deploying the software must provide the binding terms applicable to its users, customers, hosting environment, and connected providers.

Authorised use

Users may access private workspaces only with credentials issued by an authorised organisation administrator and within their assigned role.

Operational responsibility

Deploying organisations are responsible for configuring authentication, encryption keys, approved integrations, retention, provider credentials, and human approval policies.

Support decisions

SupportMemory provides evidence and workflow controls, but organisations remain responsible for customer communications and consequential operational decisions.

Availability

Availability and support commitments are determined by the organisation or service provider operating the deployment.
